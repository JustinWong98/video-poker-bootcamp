How to Play
×

Enter your bet using the buttons, and then choose which cards to keep.
They will have a border around them.

Then, press to redraw/keep. See winning hands below with their multipliers.

Combinations

  • Royal Flush: 100x
  • Straight Flush: 50x
  • Four of a Kind: 25x
  • Full House: 9x
  • Flush: 6x
  • Straight: 4x
  • Three of A Kind: 3x
  • Two Pair: 2x
